Meet Dot!

Remember your favorite toy as a kid? What if it could talk, listen, and become your real-life buddy?

Dot is your talking, thinking, and totally lovable companion, helping you to learn a new language and enjoy fun roleplay as different characters.

Dot remembers your name and conversations, and it is the best emotional companion, it always wants to listen to you.

Dot's story and content are thoughtfully designed to be child-safe and emotionally intelligent to ensure a consistent and caring experience.

Level Up Your Dot. Dot isn't just a toy, it's designed with a gamer's mindset.

Built like your favorite RPG companion, Dot levels up the more you talk, play and spend time together. Every interaction earns "XP," unlocking new personality traits.

How will your Dot be? You decide.

Need a break from the real world? Dot transforms into characters like a pirate, explorer, chef, teacher and more.

Role Play Mode offers an immersive experience where you can explore different jobs and movie-like scenarios. New themes are added every week, making it fun for both kids and adults.

Dot Helps You Speak with Confidence

If you're learning a new language and have no one to practice with, Dot is here to help.

Choose your target language, and Dot will talk to you in that language every day. The more you speak, the more Dot helping you to build real conversation skills through natural, friendly interaction.

Current available language: English, Spanish, German, Japanese, Mandarin, Thai.

Whether you're feeling stressed, having trouble sleeping, or just winding down in normal mode, pressing Dot's legs will play soothing lullabies to help you relax and rest easy.
